VELOUR SALES STRATEGY

The exact blueprint for closing high-ticket luxury web development deals.

Objective: To quickly identify and close independent luxury brands using a "Show, Don't Tell" video outreach strategy.

1. The Target Niches

Target independent brands with high margins and fast decision-making processes (no corporate red tape).

  • Premium Independent Streetwear: #archivefashion, #independentdesigner, #darkwear
  • "Slow Fashion" / Sustainable Luxury: #slowfashionbrand, #sustainableluxury
  • Niche Fragrances: #nicheperfume, #artisanperfume
  • Bespoke Jewelry: #finejewelry, #bespokejeweler

2. The "Show, Don't Tell" Pitch Method

Do not send generic emails. Create a short-form video (Instagram Reel / TikTok) or a custom screen recording for the client.

The Hook & Script

[0:00 - 0:03] Face Cam: "If you are selling clothing for more than $150, your basic Shopify theme is costing you sales."

[0:03 - 0:10] Face Cam: "Luxury buyers don't just buy the product; they buy the feeling. If your website feels cheap, your clothes feel cheap."

[0:10 - 0:25] Screen Record of VELOUR: "I just built this cinematic storefront architecture. It uses advanced smooth-scrolling and micro-animations to create an editorial experience that makes products look incredibly expensive."

[0:25 - 0:30] Face Cam: "I am taking on exactly 2 independent fashion brands this month to migrate them to this premium framework. If you want this for your brand, DM me the word 'VELOUR'."

3. The "ASAP" Outbound Strategy

Do not wait for inbound leads. Hunt them in the DMs using this exact sequence:

  1. Find 30 brands on Instagram in the target niches.
  2. Like 3 posts and leave 1 genuine comment (warms up the algorithm so you don't hit the hidden requests folder).
  3. Send the opening DM:
"Hey [Name], absolutely love the new collection. Just saw the [Product] and the design is incredible. I actually just built a high-end digital storefront specifically designed for luxury brands like yours, and I immediately thought of you guys. I shot a quick 30-second video showing how it looks—mind if I send it over?"

When they say "Sure", send the video. Then close with: "Glad you like it! I'm looking to partner with one brand this week to implement this exact framework at a steep discount to build my portfolio. Got 10 minutes tomorrow to see if it makes sense?"

4. Timezone Outreach Matrix (India Standard Time - IST)

Founders check DMs early morning, lunch, and end-of-day. Never pitch on Mondays or Friday afternoons. The golden days are Tuesday, Wednesday, and Thursday.
